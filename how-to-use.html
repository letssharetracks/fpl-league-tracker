How to Use FPL League Tracker

Track your Fantasy Premier League mini-leagues like a pro

üöÄ Getting Started

  1. Find your Team ID - Go to the FPL website, click your team, and look at the URL. It will be something like fantasy.premierleague.com/entry/123456/event/1. The number is your Team ID.
  2. Enter your Team ID - Paste it into the input box and click "Find Leagues"
  3. Select a league - Choose from your mini-leagues in the dropdown
  4. Done! - Your league data will load automatically
üí° Tip: Your Team ID is saved automatically. Next time you visit, you'll go straight to your leagues!

üìä Live View

The default view shows your league standings with live gameweek data.

Column Guide

  • GW ‚Äî Gameweek points (red = hit taken)
  • Gap ‚Äî Points difference to player above
  • Cpt ‚Äî Captain chosen this week
  • Chip ‚Äî Active chip (BB, TC, FH, WC)
  • Auto-Sub ‚Äî Predicted bench players coming on
  • BPS ‚Äî Bonus points (confirmed or provisional)
  • DEFCON ‚Äî Defensive contribution points
  • Status ‚Äî Players still to play / playing

Tap a Row for More Details

Click/tap any row to expand and see full breakdown including:

  • Captain points with multiplier
  • All bonus point earners
  • Transfer hits
  • Gap to 1st place
  • GW and Overall rank

üîÑ Auto-Subs Toggle

The Auto-Subs toggle in the top controls bar lets you toggle predicted auto-substitutions on/off.

  • ON (green): Points include predicted bench players coming on for non-playing starters
  • OFF: Shows raw points without auto-sub predictions
üí° Tip: Auto-subs are predictions based on current match status. They may change as games progress!

üìà Charts View

Click CHARTS to see visual analysis of your league.

Chart Types

  • Total: Cumulative points over the season
  • Weekly: Points scored each gameweek
  • Position: League position changes over time

Controls

  • Toggle players: Click names in the sidebar to show/hide on chart
  • Top 10 / All / Clear: Quick select buttons
  • GW range: Filter to specific gameweeks
  • Search: Find specific players

üìö Features Explained

BPS Bonus Points

Shows which of your players are earning bonus points. During matches, these are provisional based on BPS scores. After matches finish, they become confirmed.

DEFCON Defensive Contributions

Shows points earned from defensive actions like tackles, interceptions, and blocks. This FPL feature primarily rewards centre-backs and defensive midfielders for their completed defensive work during matches.

Live Status

During active matches, you'll see live indicators showing how many players are currently playing or yet to play.

‚≠ê Your Team

Your team is marked with a star so you can easily spot yourself in the league table.
