How to Use FPL League Tracker

Live gameweek tracking + season charts for your mini-leagues

🚀 Getting Started

  1. Find your Team ID - On FPL, click Points in the menu. Your ID is the number in the URL: fantasy.premierleague.com/entry/123456/event/...
  2. Enter your Team ID - Paste it into the input box and click "Find My Leagues"
  3. Select a league - Choose from your mini-leagues in the dropdown
  4. Done! - Your league data will load automatically

Works with leagues of any size — smaller leagues load all managers instantly, while larger leagues paginate smartly with a "Find Me" button to jump straight to your position.

💡 Tip: Your Team ID is saved automatically. Next time you visit, you'll go straight to your leagues!

📊 Live View

The default view shows your league standings with live gameweek data — just the essentials without the clutter.

Column Guide

  • GW — Gameweek points (red = hit taken)
  • Gap — Points difference to player above
  • Cpt — Captain chosen this week
  • Chip — Active chip (BB, TC, FH, WC)
  • Auto-Sub — Predicted bench players coming on
  • BPS — Bonus points (confirmed or provisional)
  • DEFCON — Defensive contribution points
  • Status — Players still to play / playing

Tap a Row for More Details

Click/tap any row to expand and see full breakdown including:

  • Captain points with multiplier
  • All bonus point earners
  • Transfer hits
  • Gap to 1st place
  • GW and Overall rank

Sort Columns

Click any column header to sort the table. Sortable columns include GW, Total, Captain, Chip, BPS, DEFCON, and Status.

Vice-Captain Detection

When a captain isn't playing (e.g. not in the squad), the tracker automatically detects this and shows the vice-captain taking over. The captain name appears with a strikethrough and the VC is displayed below. Points are correctly doubled (or tripled for Triple Captain) for the vice-captain across all calculations.

👥 Squad View

Tap a team name in the table to open the squad modal, showing the full starting XI and bench.

What You'll See

  • Starting players with position badges, points, and captain/VC markers
  • Bench players in order
  • Predicted auto-subs highlighted
  • Transfers made this GW with net swing (points gained/lost)
  • Free transfers remaining

Tap a Player for History

Tap any player in the squad to expand their last 5 gameweek stats. Only one player expands at a time (accordion style). Stats shown include:

  • GW / OPP — Gameweek number and opponent (H/A)
  • Goals, Assists, Bonus — Key attacking returns
  • CS — Clean sheets (GK, DEF, MID only)
  • DC — Defensive contribution points earned (tick shown when achieved)
  • SV — Saves (goalkeepers)
  • GC — Goals conceded while on pitch (GK, DEF, MID only)
  • Cards, OG, PM, PS — Yellow/red cards, own goals, penalties missed/saved
  • MIN / PTS — Minutes played and total points (colour-coded)
💡 Tip: Stat columns only appear when there's data — a striker won't show CS or GC columns since they don't affect their points. Data is cached after first tap so subsequent taps are instant.

🔄 Auto-Subs Toggle

The Auto-Subs toggle in the top controls bar lets you toggle predicted auto-substitutions on/off.

  • ON (green): Points include predicted bench players coming on for non-playing starters
  • OFF: Shows raw points without auto-sub predictions
💡 Tip: Auto-subs are predictions based on current match status. They may change as games progress!

📈 Charts View

Click CHARTS to visualise your mini-league's entire season. See who peaked early, who's been climbing since Christmas, and who's been in freefall.

Chart Types

  • Total: Cumulative points race — watch the season unfold week by week
  • Weekly: Points scored each gameweek — spot form streaks and disasters
  • Position: League position over time — relive every overtake and collapse

Sidebar Controls

  • Toggle managers: Click names in the sidebar to show/hide on chart (shows team name + manager name)
  • Top 10 / All / Clear: Quick select buttons
  • GW range: Filter to specific gameweeks to zoom in on key periods
  • Search: Find specific managers in large leagues
💡 Tip: Toggle managers on/off to compare head-to-head rivalries or see exactly when you overtook your mate!

📱 Using on Mobile

The tracker is fully responsive and works great on phones!

Portrait Mode

  • Simplified table with key columns
  • Tap any row to see full details
  • Auto-Subs toggle in top controls

Landscape Mode

  • More columns visible
  • Manager names shown under team names

📚 Features Explained

BPS Bonus Points

Shows which of your players are earning bonus points. During matches, these are provisional based on BPS scores. After matches finish, they become confirmed.

DEFCON Defensive Contributions

Shows points earned from defensive actions like tackles, interceptions, and blocks. This FPL feature primarily rewards centre-backs and defensive midfielders for their completed defensive work during matches.

Live Status

During active matches, you'll see live indicators showing how many players are currently playing or yet to play.

⭐ Your Team

Your team is marked with a star so you can easily spot yourself in the league table.

⚽ Goal Ticker

A scrolling ticker at the top shows live goals from Premier League matches.

  • Cyan = home team scorer, Yellow = away team scorer
  • Shows scorer name, minute, and assister
  • Updates automatically during live matches

💡 Pro Tips

  • Refresh button: Click to fetch latest live data during matches
  • Star a league: Click ☆ next to a league to set it as your default
  • Sort columns: Click any column header (GW, Total, Captain, Chip, BPS, DEFCON) to sort
  • Squad view: Tap a team name to see their full squad, then tap a player for their recent history
  • Reset: Click Reset to clear all cached data if something seems wrong
  • Multiple leagues: Switch between leagues using the dropdown
